The Way Forward: Practical Solutions

Establish Digital Boundaries

Implement **"Phone-Free Zones"** like the dining table and bedroom. Use device-free time slots (e.g., 7 PM onwards) to reconnect with real-world activities and loved ones.

Practice Intentional Screen Time

Turn off all non-essential **notifications**. Schedule specific times to check social media or email, rather than reacting instantly to every ping. Replace doomscrolling with mindful breathing exercises.

Cultivate Present Relationships

During family time or date night, physically **put your phone away** and silence it. Dedicate 15 minutes of uninterrupted, device-free conversation with your spouse or children daily.
